About Me

David Reel is a custom airboat salesman from Orlando, Fl. He has lived in the area since 1998 and has worked in the airboat industry since 2003. David's present responsibilities include client support and guidance with respect to new sales, as well as following up with existing and prospective clients to ensure satisfaction and gain feedback. Additionally, he writes tailored price quotes and the corresponding build specifications for complete and partial boats. David handles shipping and receiving both domestically and internationally as well as coordinating with brokers to transport boats to various countries. He attends industry specific trade shows to promote products and network with vendors. David communicates directly with the webmaster to update the website content and achieve desired styling changes. He also manages the content of the online store as well as keeps the online parts catalog current.
